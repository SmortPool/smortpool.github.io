What we Offer

Energy cosumption and finance optimization for SPA centres. We do it through mesasurements, simulations and calculations. Proposing calculation-based energy-saving methods that provide optimal inner climate. Putting methods to use using crowdfinancing and help of investors.

Energy Savings analysis

We offer a deep analysis on a spa centre to monitor current building and energy use data. Finding methods to save energy without compromising on the inner climate.

Investment-based energy efficiency projects

Invest money to help making a spa centre energy efficient. Investors receive money back from their investment %. Expected payback period up to 2 years.

Applying saving methods

Cut down the costs of the SPA centre using simulation-confirmed methods. Optimizing, replacing energy-draining devices. Making the SPA work better as a system.

Process

How do we do it? Example for one SPA centre.

  • Gathering technical info about the SPA centre

    System information and settings, costs of utilities, room plans, the measurements of pool surfaces. Using loggers and sensors to get real-time data from the SPA.

  • Finding the saving spots

    If we see potential, we use data for simulations and predictions to find out best ways to make the SPA more efficient energy-wise. Finding out concrete changes to be made and improvements to carry through if the savings plan gets funded.

  • Getting financing through investors

    As we have a plan place, we create a investment project for investors to join. If finding the funding is successful, we carry out the project to make the SPA centre more energy-efficient.

  • Carrying out the project

    We use simulation-based information and measurements to implement our solution of energy-saving methods to the spa centre. This includes modifying pool- and ventillation devices parameters and replacing devices that are keeping the costs high.

  • Payback period

    After the project has been successfully carried out, the utility bills for the spa centre are lower. From that saving, adequete share is given back to the investors who helped to make the project come true.
